Our Story

We believe that access to clean water is a basic human right. Our mission is to bring safe drinking water to every person on the planet. Since our founding in 2006, we have funded over 91,000 water projects in 29 countries, providing clean water to more than 13 million people.

Real Impact: Meet Amina

“Thanks to charity: water, Amina’s village now has access to clean, safe drinking water. What used to be a daily struggle is now a source of hope—giving her time for school, better health, and a brighter future.”
